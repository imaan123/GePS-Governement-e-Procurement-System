# GePS-Governement-e-Procurement-System

AI for Bharath 2 Hackathon Challenge. An e-procurement system that utilizes aritificial intelligence to automate bidder evaluation for procurement process
The intelligent e-Procurement System that harnesses the power of AI to bring efficiency, transparency, and scalability to government tender evaluations. By transforming how eligibility is assessed, our system aims to replace manual, error-prone processes with structured, explainable, and auditable decision-making—without removing human judgment from the loop.

**The Problem We Address**

Government procurement depends on rigorous eligibility checks to ensure that only capable and compliant bidders participate in public tenders. These checks span technical expertise, financial stability, and regulatory adherence. However, the reality is far from straightforward:
Tender documents often run into hundreds of unstructured pages, with eligibility rules buried across legal clauses, annexures, and technical tables.
Bidder submissions are equally chaotic—ranging from typed PDFs and scanned certificates to blurry images of stamped documents, inconsistent tables, and handwritten notes.
Procurement officers must manually extract, interpret, and cross-check every rule against every document—a process that is slow, exhausting, and prone to oversight.
The complexity deepens when rules interact: mandatory AND conditions, alternative OR clauses, bidder-type-specific rules, and overriding dependencies. Add ambiguous or poorly scanned documents, subjective judgments, and limited traceability, and the risk of inconsistency, delay, and audit failure grows significantly.

**Why It Matters**

For governments, procurement transparency is non-negotiable. Every eligibility decision must be explainable, defensible, and backed by clear evidence. Yet manual evaluation struggles to meet these demands at scale.

**Our Solution in Brief**

We are building a system architecture designed to turn chaotic documents into structured, auditable decisions:

Tender Processing Pipeline: Extracts and organizes eligibility criteria from complex, multi-section tenders into structured rule sets.

Bidder Document Processing Pipeline: Standardizes heterogeneous submissions (PDFs, images, Word files, tables) using OCR and layout analysis to create a structured bidder profile.

Rule Matching Engine: Compares bidder data against rules, evaluating each criterion independently, handling logical dependencies, and flagging low-confidence cases for manual review.

Human Review & Audit Interface: Empowers procurement officers to validate ambiguous cases, override decisions, add justifications, and maintain a complete, traceable audit trail.

**The Outcome**

A scalable, explainable, and human-in-the-loop AI system that accelerates procurement, reduces human error, ensures consistency, and upholds the highest standards of public accountability.
